SWAN-DF database of audio-video deepfakes

We present SWAN-DF database of high quality audio-visual deepfake that pose a high threat to state of the art speaker and face verification systems. We selected 46 different identities from a publicly available SWAN database and manually matched 60 pairs of people for face swapping process. For face swapping, we used several tuned models from DeepFaceLab and for voice conversion a tuned model from FreeVC. We generated more than 20 face swapping variants using the combination of different models and blending techniques.

For each pair of original videos, you can see 4 examples of deepfake variants where the faces and voices of the original identities are swapped. Below you can also listen to other examples of audio-only deepfakes generated using original utterances from LibriTTS.
